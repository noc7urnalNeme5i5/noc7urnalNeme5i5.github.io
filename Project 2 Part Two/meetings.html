Meetings and Events

Meetings

The M.U. Anime Club meets twice a week on Thursday and Friday. All meetings are held in 111 Harrison Hall unless stated otherwise below.

Thursday Night Anime

Thursdays at 7pm ~ 9pm we rotate between 5 weekly events, which do not require any prior experience with the club to enjoy.

  • Week 1: Anime discussion - We get together and discuss topics about anime. This meeting will take place in 210 Harrison Hall.

  • Week 2: Anime of the Week - Members choose the anime they would like to watch. then we watch 4-5 episodes of the anime.

  • Week 3: Game Night - Play games that are from Japan and/or anime related. This meeting will take place in 012 Harrison Hall.

  • Week 4: Movie Night - A member chosen movie is shown.

  • Week 5: Old School Night - Members choose the anime they would like to watch from a selection of anime greater than 10 years old.

Friday Night Anime

During this meeting we watch one episode of each of the anime listed in the lineup each week. If you have not been here from the first meeting of the semester it is recommended that you watch these anime up to the episode the club is on, as it could be confusing otherwise.

Friday Anime Lineup

  • Log Horizon

  • One Punch Man

  • Death Parade

  • Noragami Aragato

  • Steins; Gate

  • Ojiisan to Marshmallow
