About

MUAnime is a similar interest based organization that hopes to bring together enthusiasts of Japanese animation at Miami University to promote sharing and discussion of this genre. This will be accomplished in weekly meetings where we play games, discuss the medium, and watch anime voted on by our members. We also often go uptown on Fridays to eat after club so feel free to join us and get to know the members even better. Be sure to contact an officer about joining the listserv so you can get the most accurate, up to the minute updates on meeting changes and special events!
